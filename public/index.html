Murder Mystery

Welcome to Murder Mystery, the interactive party game where you and your friends will try to solve a murder — or get away with it.

How to play

The host will choose a case file. Then, each player is assigned a character with a backstory and a secret. One of you is the killer, and their job is to keep their identity hidden.

Everyone else must figure out who the killer is. Good luck!
